**Problem Statement**
Blood donation is a critical healthcare service that saves millions of lives annually. However, the process of determining donor eligibility is often manual, time-consuming, and prone to human error. Many potential donors are unaware of the comprehensive medical criteria required for safe blood donation, leading to:

1)Inefficient screening processes at blood donation camps

2)Lack of awareness among potential donors about eligibility requirements

3)Inconsistent application of medical criteria across different donation centers

4)Time wastage for both donors and healthcare staff when ineligible donors arrive

5)Potential health risks to both donors and recipients when proper screening isn't followed

**Scope of the Project**
   **In-Scope:**
Pre-donation eligibility assessment for potential blood donors

Comprehensive medical criteria validation based on standard blood donation guidelines

User-friendly interface for donor registration and screening

Automated eligibility determination with clear pass/fail criteria

Educational component about donation requirements and precautions
**Out-of-Scope:**
Actual blood donation appointment scheduling

Integration with hospital/blood bank databases

Medical professional verification of provided information

Physical examination components

Donor-recipient matching

**Target Users**
1)Potential Blood Donors : First-time donors seeking eligibility information or Regular donors checking current eligibility status
2)Blood Banks & Healthcare Institutions : Pre-screening tool to reduce administrative workload and Educational resource for donor awareness programs
3)Blood Donation Camp Organizers :Streamlined donor pre-registration process
4)Healthcare NGOs : Community awareness and donor recruitment tool

**Technical Specifications**
Platform: Python-based console application

Data Handling: In-memory data storage with validation

User Interface: Text-based interactive console
